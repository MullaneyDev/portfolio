Why Code Nation?

For the last 10 years I have been working within the education sector as a Teaching assistant and then a Teacher.
After the birth of my two children, I have made the decision to change my career path so that I can spend more time mentally with my family.
Coding is something I have always been interested in and as part of my last job I was the computing lead for the school.
This allowed me to become more involved with the education of young people and their interactions with the digital world.
I implemented a new coding curriculum as well as investing in new technology to allow the children to become better equiped for the future.

I have a friend who completed the Code Nation:Master Software course last year and he recommended it to me as a way to pursue my interests.
His experiences were very positive and made it an easy decision for me about my course provider.
